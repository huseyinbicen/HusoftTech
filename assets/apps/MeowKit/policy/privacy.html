Privacy Policy

Effective date: February 2026

1. Introduction

MeowKit ("the App") cares about your privacy and data security. This document explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal and cat-related information when you use MeowKit: Cat Care.

2. Data Collection

Personal Data

  • We collect your email address and authentication details only if you create an account or subscribe to premium features.
  • Your personal information is used for login, cloud sync, and managing your subscription status.

Cat Data

  • Cat profiles, photos, weight records, vaccination details, feeding schedules, voice recordings, and notes are stored locally on your device.
  • If you are a premium user and signed in, this data is securely synced to the cloud (Firebase/Firestore). Photos and audio are uploaded to Firebase Storage for safe backup.

Usage Data

  • MeowKit may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve app stability, features, and user experience.
  • No personal or cat data is shared with third parties or advertisers.

3. Data Usage

  • Your information is used exclusively to deliver app features: cat management, care reminders, notifications, and – for premium users – secure cloud sync and backup.
  • We never sell, trade, or disclose your personal or cat data to external parties.

4. Data Storage and Security

  • All data is securely stored on your device by default.
  • For premium users, encrypted data is transmitted securely and stored in Firebase/Firestore under user-specific paths.
  • Cat photos and voice clips are cached locally and, if premium, backed up to cloud storage.
  • The app uses industry-standard security practices to protect your data both in transit and at rest.

5. Notifications

  • MeowKit uses device notifications to remind you about vaccines, feeding times, and birthdays.
  • You control notification permissions in your device settings and can enable or disable reminders for each cat.

6. User Rights

  • You may delete your account and all linked data at any time from the Profile tab.
  • To request a copy of your data or request deletion, contact us at: husofttech@gmail.com.
  • After account deletion, all personal and cat data will be permanently erased from the app and, if applicable, from the cloud.

7. Children's Privacy

  • MeowKit is not intended for children under 13 years old.
  • We do not knowingly collect or store personal data from children under 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

  • This privacy policy may be updated over time.
  • If significant changes are made, we will provide notice within the app or on our website.

9. Contact

  • For privacy or data-related questions or concerns, please contact: husofttech@gmail.com

App Store Policy Statement

  • MeowKit does not require user registration for basic features.
  • All personal and cat-related data is stored securely and never sold or shared.
  • Premium features (cloud sync, unlimited cats, advanced reminders) require authentication and a subscription.
  • The app uses local notifications for reminders; notification access requires user consent and control.
  • Users can delete their account and all app data at any time within the app settings.

*For any privacy or data removal requests, contact: husofttech@gmail.com*
