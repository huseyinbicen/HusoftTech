Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 25, 2026

Thank you for using NorthPin: Compass ("NorthPin", "we", "our", or "the app").

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the NorthPin mobile application.

1. Data Collection

NorthPin does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data.

  • We do not require account creation.
  • We do not use analytics services.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not track users.

All app data is stored locally on your device.

2. Location Information

NorthPin uses your device's location services to:

  • Determine compass accuracy
  • Calculate direction alignment
  • Support saved target functionality
  • Provide altitude and bearing calculations

Location data is processed locally on your device and is never transmitted to us or any third party.

3. Saved Targets and Preferences

Any targets, coordinates, settings, and preferences you create are stored locally on your device.

We do not have access to this data.

4. In-App Purchases

NorthPin offers a one-time Pro upgrade through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

  • All payments are processed securely by Apple. We do not receive or store payment information.
  • Purchase status is managed by Apple and linked to your Apple ID.

5. Third-Party Services

NorthPin does not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising networks, or tracking services.

6. Data Security

Since we do not collect or store user data on external servers, there is no remote storage of personal information.

All app data remains on your device.

7. Children's Privacy

NorthPin does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality changes. Updates will be reflected with a revised "Last updated" date.

9. Contact

For privacy or data-related questions or concerns, please contact: husofttech@gmail.com
