Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 1, 2026

Thank you for using GlobePaper Wallpapers ("GlobePaper", "we", "our", or "the app"). This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

GlobePaper Wallpapers is designed to operate without user accounts.

  • No account registration is required.
  • We do not collect personal information.
  • We do not collect contact details, identifiers, or location data.
  • We do not use advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use third-party tracking technologies.
  • We do not sell or share user data.

2. Local App Storage

App preferences are stored locally on your Mac using standard macOS system storage mechanisms (such as UserDefaults and app cache).

This may include:

  • Selected wallpaper provider
  • Scheduling configuration
  • Market or region selection
  • Cache limit settings
  • Last update timestamp

All stored settings remain on your device.

3. Wallpaper Content and Network Access

GlobePaper downloads wallpaper metadata and images from selected providers (such as Bing and NASA APOD).

  • Network access is required to fetch wallpaper content.
  • Downloaded images are saved in the app cache directory on your device.
  • Cached files are automatically cleaned based on your configured cache limit.

We do not transmit personal data to wallpaper providers.

4. File Export

If you choose to export a wallpaper, GlobePaper uses the standard macOS file selection dialog.

  • Files are saved only to locations you explicitly select.
  • The app does not access files outside user-approved locations.

5. Analytics and Diagnostics

GlobePaper does not include third-party analytics, advertising tracking, or behavioral monitoring tools.

Apple may collect anonymous diagnostic information under its own privacy policies when you download or use apps from the Mac App Store.

6. Children's Privacy

GlobePaper is not directed to children under the age of 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

7. Data Security

Downloaded wallpapers and settings remain on your device unless you manually export files or back up your system.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if app functionality or legal requirements change.

Updates will be reflected by revising the "Last updated" date above.
