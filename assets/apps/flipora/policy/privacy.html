Flipora: Clock

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Flipora: Clock ("Flipora", "we", "our", or "the app") is a flip clock, alarm, timer, world clock, focus, and widget app designed to work primarily on-device. We do not collect personal data.

1. Data Collection

Flipora does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No analytics SDKs are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • No user tracking is performed.
  • No personal data is collected by us.

2. On-Device Data Only

Flipora stores app data locally on your device to support core functionality.

This may include:

  • clock display preferences
  • theme and background selections
  • alarm and timer settings
  • display protection options
  • world clock selections
  • focus progress and history
  • widget-related app state
  • Pro unlock status as provided by Apple

We do not have access to this data.

3. In-App Purchases

Flipora includes optional Pro features unlocked through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

  • Payments are processed securely by Apple.
  • We do not receive or store your full payment details.
  • Purchase entitlement is associated with your Apple ID under Apple's policies.

4. Notifications and System Integrations

Flipora may request permission to send notifications for alarms and timers.

The app may also use Apple system features such as:

  • widgets
  • Live Activities
  • local device storage

These features operate using on-device app state and do not send personal data to us.

5. No Account or Cloud Requirement

Flipora does not require account registration or sign-in to use its core features.

6. Children's Privacy

Flipora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if Flipora features change. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.
