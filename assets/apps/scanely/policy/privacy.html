Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

Scanely ("Scanely", "we", "our", or "the app") is a privacy-first QR and barcode reader and creator for iPhone and iPad. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the app.

1. Data Collection

Scanely does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information with us or third parties.

  • No account creation or sign-in is required.
  • No analytics SDKs are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • No user tracking is performed.
  • We do not receive your scan history, created codes, or saved items.

2. On-Device Processing

Live camera frames and imported photos are decoded on-device using Apple frameworks, including Vision and VisionKit where applicable.

  • Camera frames are used only to detect and read codes in real time.
  • Imported photos are processed locally on your device.
  • We do not upload camera content, photos, or decoded results to our servers.

3. Local Storage

Scanely stores app data locally on your device to provide core functionality.

This may include:

  • scan history
  • saved codes and created codes
  • favorites, folders, categories, notes, and preferences
  • theme, widget, and safety settings
  • Pro unlock status as provided by Apple

We do not have access to this locally stored data.

4. Optional iCloud Sync

Scanely Pro may offer optional iCloud sync for Saved and Created items through Apple's CloudKit infrastructure.

  • Sync is off by default and only happens if you enable it.
  • Synced data is stored in your private iCloud database under your Apple account.
  • We do not access, review, or control your private iCloud data.
  • You can disable sync at any time from within the app or your device settings.

5. Permissions

Scanely may request access to certain Apple system permissions to support app features.

  • Camera access is used for live scanning.
  • Photo library access is used only when you choose to import or export images.

You can revoke these permissions at any time in iOS Settings.

6. External Actions

Scanely can present actions based on scanned content, such as opening a website, placing a call, composing an email, joining a Wi-Fi network, or opening a map location.

These actions occur only when you explicitly choose them. Any network activity that follows is handled by Apple system apps or the external services you choose to open.

7. In-App Purchases

Scanely offers optional Pro features through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

  • Payments are processed securely by Apple.
  • We do not receive or store your full payment details.
  • Purchase entitlement is managed by Apple and linked to your Apple ID.

8. Children's Privacy

Scanely does not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if Scanely's features or data handling change. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, contact: husofttech@gmail.com
