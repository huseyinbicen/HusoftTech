Flipora: Clock

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 10, 2026

Flipora: Clock ("Flipora", "we", "our", or "the app") is designed as a simple clock experience. We do not collect personal data.

1. Data Collection

Flipora does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal information.

  • No account creation is required.
  • No analytics SDKs are used.
  • No advertising SDKs are used.
  • No user tracking is performed.

2. On-Device Data Only

App settings and content such as clock layout preferences, theme choices, alarm/timer settings, and display-protection options are stored locally on your device.

We do not have access to this data.

3. In-App Purchases

Flipora includes optional Pro features unlocked through Apple's In-App Purchase system.

  • Payments are handled securely by Apple.
  • We do not receive or store your payment details.
  • Purchase status is tied to your Apple ID per Apple's policies.

4. Notifications and System Integrations

Flipora may use iOS notification permissions for alarms and timers, and supports widgets and Live Activities using on-device app state.

These features do not send personal data to us.

5. Children's Privacy

Flipora does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy if Flipora features change. Any updates will be published on this page with a revised "Last updated" date.

7. Contact

Questions about privacy? Contact husofttech@gmail.com.
